Child Safety Standards

CatKats is committed to protecting children and preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) on our platform.

Our Commitment

CatKats takes child safety seriously and has implemented comprehensive measures to prevent, detect, and respond to child sexual abuse material (CSAM) and exploitation on our dating platform.

Safety Measures

  • Age Verification: Users must be 18+ to use our platform
  • Content Moderation: Automated and manual review of all shared content including photos, messages, and profiles
  • Reporting System: Easy-to-use in-app reporting for safety concerns and inappropriate behavior
  • User Blocking: Users can block and report inappropriate behavior immediately
  • Content Filtering: AI-powered detection of inappropriate content and images
  • Account Suspension: Immediate action against policy violations and suspicious accounts
  • Profile Verification: Enhanced verification processes for user profiles
  • Message Monitoring: Automated scanning of messages for inappropriate content

Reporting Child Safety Concerns

If you encounter any content or behavior that may involve child sexual abuse or exploitation:

  • Use our in-app reporting feature immediately
  • Contact us directly at the email below
  • Report to local authorities if necessary
  • Block the user immediately to prevent further contact

Content Policy

CatKats strictly prohibits:

  • Sharing or requesting child sexual abuse material
  • Grooming or sexual communication with minors
  • Any form of child exploitation or abuse
  • Inappropriate content involving minors
  • Fake profiles claiming to be minors
  • Any attempt to contact or meet with minors

Compliance

CatKats complies with all relevant child safety laws and regulations, including:

  • Reporting to regional and national authorities
  • Cooperation with law enforcement investigations
  • Regular safety audits and updates
  • Staff training on child safety protocols
  • Data retention policies for safety investigations
  • International cooperation on child safety matters
